Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: Singapore

This Cookie Policy explains how lokalads uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform. Our use of cookies complies with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our platform. They help us deliver a secure, functional, and personalised experience. Cookies cannot be used to execute programs or transmit viruses.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Essential for the platform to function. These cannot be disabled.

  • session_id — Maintains your active login session
  • csrf_token — Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • country_pref — Stores your country selection (Singapore)

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences to personalise your experience.

  • lang_pref — Stores your language preference (English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil)
  • search_history — Remembers recent searches for quicker access

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our platform. Data collected is aggregated and anonymised. We obtain your consent before setting these cookies.

  • _ga, _gid — Google Analytics (if consented)
  • la_analytics — Our internal usage analytics

3. Managing Cookies

You may manage cookie preferences via:

  • Our cookie consent banner on your first visit
  • Our Cookie Settings, accessible at any time in the footer
  • Your browser settings to block or delete cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some platform features may use third-party cookies from providers such as Google. These third parties operate under their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via our platform or by email.
