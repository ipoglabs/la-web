Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: Singapore

This Privacy Policy explains how lokalads ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data. This policy complies with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore.

1. Data Collected

  • Identity data: name, NRIC/FIN (where required for verification), username
  • Contact data: email address, Singapore mobile number, postal code
  • Transaction data: listing details, enquiries, payment records
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, browser, pages visited
  • Location data: district or area when searching or posting listings

2. Purpose of Collection

We collect your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our platform services
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To send transactional notifications and service updates
  • To comply with the PDPA and other applicable Singapore laws
  • To personalise search results and content based on your preferences

3. Disclosure of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose your data to:

  • Service providers engaged to support our platform operations
  • Singapore government agencies or law enforcement where required by law
  • Business partners, with your prior written consent

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as required by Singapore law. Upon account closure, data is deleted or anonymised within 60 days, unless a statutory obligation requires longer retention.

5. Your Rights Under the PDPA

You have the right to:

  • Access — request the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for optional data uses at any time
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@lokalads.sg.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security arrangements including encryption, access controls, and regular audits to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, copying, or modification.

7. Transfer Outside Singapore

If we transfer your personal data outside Singapore, we ensure the recipient provides a standard of protection comparable to the PDPA, through contractual arrangements or other means.

8. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated with at least 30 days' notice via email or a platform notice.
