Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: United Kingdom

This Privacy Policy explains how lokalads ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. This policy complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. lokalads is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

1. Data Controller

lokalads Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through this platform. Our registered address is in England and Wales (Company Registration No. 8765412345).

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity data: first name, last name, username
  • Contact data: email address, phone number, billing address
  • Transaction data: details of listings, purchases, and payments
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, time zone, cookie data
  • Location data: your general location when searching or posting listings

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract: to provide services you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: to improve our platform and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation: to comply with UK law
  • Consent: for marketing communications (you can withdraw at any time)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g. cloud hosting, payment processors) under data processing agreements
  • UK law enforcement or regulatory bodies where legally required
  • Successor entities in the event of a business transfer

5. International Transfers

If we transfer your data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including UK-approved standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected, or as required by law. Account data is deleted within 30 days of a closure request, subject to statutory obligations.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lokalads.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes with at least 30 days' notice. Continued use of our platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
