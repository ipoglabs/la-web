Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: India

This Privacy Policy explains how lokalads ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. This policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identity data: name, username, date of birth
  • Contact data: email address, mobile number, postal address
  • Transaction data: details of listings posted, enquiries made, payments processed
  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Location data: city, state, or pincode when you search or post listings

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our platform and services
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To send you transactional emails and service notifications
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under Indian law
  • To personalise your experience based on your location and search history

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g. cloud hosting, payment processors)
  • Law enforcement agencies where required by Indian law or court order
  • Business partners with your explicit consent

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as required by applicable Indian laws. After account closure, data is deleted or anonymised within 90 days, unless a longer retention period is mandated by law.

5. Your Rights Under DPDPA

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Nominate another individual to exercise your rights on your behalf
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our platform. Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
