Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: India

This Cookie Policy explains how lokalads uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our platform. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the platform to function correctly. They enable core features such as security, account login, and session management. These cookies cannot be disabled.

  • session_id — Maintains your login session
  • csrf_token — Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • country_pref — Remembers your selected country (India)

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and personalise your experience.

  • lang_pref — Stores your language preference
  • search_history — Remembers recent searches for quicker access

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform, allowing us to improve performance and usability. Data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

  • _ga, _gid — Google Analytics (if enabled)
  • la_analytics — Our internal usage analytics

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies currently stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our platform. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be blocked without affecting core features.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some features on our platform may use third-party cookies from providers such as Google or payment processors. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our platform.
