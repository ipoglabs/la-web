# About Me

## Who I Am
UX Designer · Mid-level UX/Frontend Developer · Visionary Product Owner / Manager

---

## Design Philosophy
- **Super simple, straight-forward experience — always.** If it needs explaining, it needs redesigning.
- Clean, bold aesthetic with strong visual hierarchy
- No clutter. No clever-for-its-own-sake.
- Every pixel must earn its place
- Purposeful, confident design over decorative complexity
- Intuitive first — the best UX is the one the user never notices

---

## How I Work
- Think in user journeys, not screens
- Validate ideas by building — POCs over presentations
- Decisions are product decisions, not just design decisions
- Simple solutions scale. Complex ones break.

---

## Code Style
- Tailwind utility-first — no custom CSS unless unavoidable
- Component-focused, readable over clever
- Minimal abstractions — only when clearly needed
- Ship it clean the first time

---

## What This Workspace Is
`poc-next` is my personal playground for:
- UX concept experiments (self-contained, navigable)
- Component snapshots and design system snippets
- High-fidelity POCs that can become real product features

---

## Guiding Principle
> **"Less, but better."**
> Every decision traces back to that — fewer steps, fewer words, fewer elements,
> but each one doing its job with complete confidence.
